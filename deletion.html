Lyncyap Account Deletion Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. How to Delete Your Account

Users may request account deletion via the app or by contacting us at elkanamagusi901@gmail.com. Include your username and email in the request.

2. What Happens After Deletion

  • Your posts, comments, messages, and profile information will be permanently removed from Lyncyap.
  • Media stored in Cloudinary associated with your account will also be deleted.
  • Backups stored in MongoDB Atlas may remain for a short period for security and auditing purposes, then permanently deleted.

3. Limitations

  • Content shared with other users may remain visible if it was shared before deletion.
  • We are not responsible for copies saved outside Lyncyap by other users.

4. Contact

If you have questions about account deletion, email: elkanamagusi901@gmail.com
