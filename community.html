Lyncyap Community Guidelines

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Be Respectful

  • Do not post hate speech or harassment.
  • Treat others with respect, even if you disagree.

2. No Illegal Content

  • Do not upload or share illegal material, including copyrighted content without permission.

3. Safe Sharing

  • Only share content you have the right to post.
  • Do not spam or flood other users with unwanted messages.

4. Reporting & Blocking

  • You may report any post or user violating the rules.
  • You may block users you don’t want to interact with.

5. Moderation

Lyncyap may remove content or suspend accounts that violate these guidelines.

6. Respect Privacy

  • Do not share someone else’s personal information without consent.

7. Changes

We may update these guidelines to keep the community safe.
