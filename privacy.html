Privacy Policy for Lyncyap

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address
  • Username

  • Reports and messages

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts

Lyncyap may collect GPS location data to enhance user experience. Location data is not sold to third parties.

4. Data Storage

User data is stored securely using MongoDB Atlas. Media files are stored using Cloudinary. We use JWT authentication to protect accounts.

5. Third-Party Services

Users may:

  • Delete their account

Users may share posts to other apps. Lyncyap is not responsible for how third-party apps use shared content.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your information.
